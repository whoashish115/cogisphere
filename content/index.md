Hi! I'm **[whoashish115](https://whoashish.com)**, and this is my personal **Computational Intelligence** wiki.

I built **Cogisphere** to keep everything I learn about intelligent systems in one place - from machine learning, deep learning, and artificial intelligence to reinforcement learning, computer vision, natural language processing, generative AI, robotics, and everything in between. Along the way, I also collect mathematical foundations, research notes, implementation guides, code snippets, papers, resources, and small insights that I don't want to forget. Instead of searching the internet for the same concepts repeatedly, I can simply come back here.

If you're visiting for the first time, I'd recommend starting with the sidebar navigation. Everything is organized by topic, making it easy to explore mathematics, optimization, probability, machine learning, deep learning, neural networks, reinforcement learning, computer vision, natural language processing, large language models, generative AI, multimodal AI, AI agents, robotics, evolutionary computation, fuzzy systems, swarm intelligence, knowledge representation, MLOps, papers, implementations, datasets, tools, and more.

Most of these notes are written for my future self, but if they help you understand a concept, reproduce a paper, build a model, or discover something new, that's even better. **Cogisphere** is a living knowledge base that will continue to grow as I learn, experiment, and explore the ever-evolving world of computational intelligence.
